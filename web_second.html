Department of Computer Science

About Us

The Department of Computer Science is committed to excellence in research, teaching, and service. Our programs provide students with a strong foundation in computer science theory and practice, preparing them for successful careers in industry, academia, and research.

Our Vision

We aim to be a leader in computer science education, driving innovation and contributing to the technological advancements that shape the future. Our students and faculty engage in cutting-edge research, pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science.

Courses Offered

  • Introduction to Programming
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Operating Systems
  • Database Management Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
